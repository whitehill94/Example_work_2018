        We are simply a community of Jesus-followers who prefer people to programs, worship-in- action to worship-in-a-building, and relationships to religion. We’re deeply committed to working together in God’s mission in Christ to bring peace and hope and love to the world – including our own world in Waimea as well as the whole world out there. We think this is being church as it was intended. We exemplify good community by looking after each other and extending care to our neighbors. We devote time together each week to meet over a meal, to celebrate the goodness of God, to understand the Scriptures, and pray together. Generosity, hospitality, faith, sacrifice, teaching, service, worship, prayer, laughter, justice- seeking, family, love, grace etc. That is the stuff of relationships that Voyage wants to foster in a Christ community. We are a community of people who share a passion for life, creativity, justice and God's Word, not perfect, just decidedly on a voyage to discover God. We seek to develop genuine Christian spirituality that connects deeply through place and space, beyond just Sundays. We do, however, meet weekly on Sunday mornings on Church Row in the old blue roofed baptist building, and hold a number of other events, gatherings and projects
